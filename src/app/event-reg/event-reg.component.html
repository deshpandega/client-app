DESCRIPTION
Every year we set new goals and want to accomplish them next year, and every year, in most cases, we fail. How do we set our new year resolutions and execute them without failure?
In this Social Cup Workshop, you will learn:
1. How to paint dream home in acrylic
2. How to set manageable goals and stick to them as we carry out daily tasks in the new year
3. How to track our progress and adjust our goals
This event is proudly brought to you by
The Social Cups ( In Art & Play We Trust)
The Social Cups organizes weekly art and creative events to help people build self-confidence, appreciation, and to facilitate deeper and meaningful inter-personal connections through art and common interests.
